DISCOVERY HOLIDAY

Our discovery holidays are designed for our applicants who are seriously considering retiring, relocating, or investing in the Riviera Maya. The discovery holiday is intended to educated you on how to safely purchase real estate in Mexico as a foreign buyer. As a licensed real estate broker VRM takes this part of the buying process extremely seriously.


"An Educated Consumer is our Best Customer". We have our finger on the pulse of the market and will guide you through the “It” developments that are producing the maximum Returns on Investment (ROI).

Our relationships with the most reputable and professional vacation rentals and property management companies in area ensure your property is protected and performing at its peak. We at VRM are not just brokers, We are Expats who own and develop in the region.

We understand the market
and we understand you.

What comes with the Discovery Weekend:

• 3 days and 2 nights stay.

· A guided tour with a VRM licensed real estate agent of the area’s established as well as in vogue developments.

· A free consultation with a licensed attorney within the top local law firm.

· Meet and Greet with (2) vacation rentals and property management companies

· Virgin Exclusive: A Comprehensive Guide to Rental Property Investing in the Riviera Maya - How to create wealth, cash flow and residual income through wise real estate investing

Join us for 3 days 2 nights to experience the lifestyle of the Riviera Maya and where the premier rental properties are producing the highest returns on investment. You will not regret this once in a lifetime experience with Virgin Realty Mexico.
