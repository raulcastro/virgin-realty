Trinity Tulum - Condos for Sale in Tulum

Trinity Tulum is the only luxury development in Tulum that combines true elegance of avant-garde, yet chic minimalist architecture with all the comforts of modern eco-living.

At the heart of our philosophy is the Trinity of three elements that are essential for complete relaxation and true enjoyment of Tulum living: the unity of unprecedented comfort, unique lifestyle and complete harmony with nature.

THE PROJECT

Trinity Tulum is designed to offer the superior functionality of a luxurious contemporary space, while at the same time being a natural part of the surroundings - thanks to its large private terraces, its airy and lofty feel, and ample, open common spaces that are an extension of the natural landscape.

RESIDENCES

Trinity Tulum's beautiful three levels feature luxury 1-Bedroom + 1-Bathroom and 2-Bedroom + 2-Bathroom residences, as well as double-level Duplex Lofts. All of the residences have the ability to contribute to the hotel rental program, therefore generating fantastic returns for the owners. 2nd and 3rd ﬂoor apartments feature spacious private terraces with private whirlpools, with access to a common rooftop area with a pool, bar and BBQ.
