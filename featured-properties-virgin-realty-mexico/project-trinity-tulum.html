WELCOME TO TRINITY TULUM

Trinity Tulum is the only luxury development in Tulum that combines true elegance of avant-garde, yet chic minimalist architecture with all the comforts of modern eco-living.
Trinity Tulum is designed to offer the superior functionality of a luxurious contemporary space, while at the same time being a natural part of the surroundings - thanks to its large private terraces, its airy and lofty feel, and ample, open common spaces that are an extension of the natural landscape.

The Trinity concept embodies our appreciation for the three main elements of nature: earth (natural, locally-sourced materials and respect for the environment), air (light, airy, open spaces reflected in the architectural design), and water (crystal clear waters of the Caribbean sea just a stone’s throw from the development’s location).

OUR PHILOSOPHY

The Trinity concept embodies our appreciation for the three main elements of nature: earth (natural, locally-sourced materials and respect for the environment), air (light, airy, open spaces reflected in the architectural design), and water (crystal clear waters of the Caribbean sea just a stone’s throw from the development’s location).
Trinity Tulum was inspired by Nature. For the design, we used simple, yet elegant forms. Our large, open common spaces are almost an extension of the natural landscape. We use only sustainably and locally sourced natural wood and stone – the same materials that were known to and used by the ancient Maya.
Air is an integral part of the Trinity Tulum design concept. Our ample common spaces let the air and light in, while every single apartment features a large private terrace with a whirlpool tub, allowing you to enjoy the tropical outside in the privacy of your home. The interior of each unit feels light, open and lofty.

RESIDENCES

Trinity Tulum's beautiful boutique development features (6) 2-Bedroom/2-Bathroom Suites and (6) 2.5-Bedroom/2-Bathroom Lofts as our signature Duplex Jungle Lofts.
The ground floor jungle lofts come with their own garden and private pool for superlative privacy and maximum rental value. All of the residences have the ability to contribute to the hotel rental program, therefore generating fantastic returns for the owners. All apartments feature spacious private terraces, some with private whirlpools, with access to the Sky Deck rooftop pool area, private movie theatre lounge, and ground floor lounge, bar and BBQ.
