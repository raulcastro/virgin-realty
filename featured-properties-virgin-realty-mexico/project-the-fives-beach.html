THE FIVES PLAYA DEL CARMEN

The 475 oversized luxury rooms are tastefully designed in contemporary architectural style. Guests will enjoy lush tropical gardens which frame views of both the Caribbean Sea and the hotel’s many crystal clear winding pools, and of the nearby cenote and mesmerizing view of the nearby mangrove, often visited by our local troop of Spider Monkeys.

For additional convenience all buildings at The Fives Hotel and Luxury Residences are equipped with elevators.

All accommodations are provided with air conditioning, LCD flat screen cable television with DVD player, iPod dock, either a king size or two double size beds, night table, radio/alarm clock, deluxe bathrobes, safety deposit box, amenity kit, Fridge, iron and ironing board, coffee maker, and direct dial telephone. Bathrooms feature a rain shower, double sinks with marble countertops, bath amenities, a hair dryer and a magnifying vanity mirror.

All suites also feature a balcony or a terrace.

THE FIVES HOTEL & LUXURY RESIDENCES

4 DIAMOND RESORT

The hotel is strategically located in Playa del Carmen on the Riviera Maya, just 35 minutes south of Cancun International Airport. With this great location, guests can experience convenience first hand with the charming restaurants, shops and local attractions of downtown Playa del Carmen, just 10-minutes away.

The area provides many other popular attractions, including the notable selection of world class exclusive Championship 18 hole golf courses only minutes away.
