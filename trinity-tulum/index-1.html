a few words

about us

The Bus and Coach Hire is a Community Interest Company founded in 2007 to develop a new kind of bus service.

In the world of Bus and Coach Hire, bus travel is fun, friendly, affordable and sustainable. In a nutshell, we aim to develop a bus service which people enjoy, look forward to, and above all, choose to use in preference to private motor transport. This way we can encourage the shift from cars to shared transport and greatly reduce the negative effects of transport on our environment and on our health and wellbeing.
