Alex Aquino is a Broker Associate and Team Leader at Virgin Realty Mexico, Alex was born in Guadalajara but grew up in Cancun, Mexico with a family background in real estate. After graduating from one of the top universities in Mexico, Alex then went on to live in Europe for several years learning three languages and immersing himself in the culture.

Since joining Virgin Realty Mexico, Alex has prided himself for his passionate and heartfelt drive to serve his clients’ best interests. He is dedicated, knowledgeable, and committed to finding the perfect fit for his clientele - whether it’s a lifestyle choice or for pure investment.

His devotion and unparalleled customer service has resulted in a continuously expanding network of loyal clients and referrals, not only in Mexico but also in Europe, Canada, and the USA. Passionate for his craft, he continuously seeks to stay ahead of the game when it comes to market education and trends.

Whether he is traveling abroad to solidify new relationships or enjoying the beach with new clients, you can find Alex constantly making new friends and engaging in the latest buzz. A perpetual student of life, he loves making new friends, presenting the newest opportunities to his clients, and of course, enjoying this incredible place we all have come to love and call home.
