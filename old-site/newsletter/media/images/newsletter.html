Why is everyone talking about Tulum, Mexico?

Tulum has become the premier destination for celebrities, artists, fashionistas and foodies alike.

Major publications such as Forbes, NY Times, Conde Naste Traveller, Travel and Leisure, and Trip Advisor are all raving about Tulum. Hosting some of the world’s most beautiful beaches, turquoise blue Caribbean water, intimate bohemian style boutique hotels positioned right on the sand, 5 Star restaurants with menus that will make your mouth water, and iconic historical archaeology.

It doesn't stop there, venture off the beaten path and you will find that Tulum has some of the world’s most breathtaking natural lagoons where you can find manatees and dolphins and don’t forget about those magical watering holes called cenotes.


Looking for activities?



The 2nd largest reef in the world is a 5 minute panga (small boat) ride away for exceptional scuba or snorkeling.

In the early mornings the sea is calm perfect for paddle boarding, there is nothing like a Caribbean sunrise, make sure to keep your eyes peeled for sea turtles and eagle rays.

If you are feeling adventurous the mid-day wind picks up around 1pm and you can try out the fast-paced sport of kiteboarding, there are several kiteboarding school scattered up and down the beach.


Why has Tulum become so attractive?




Besides the obvious mentioned above, Tulum has become a world class destination due to its accessibility". Just a 3-5 hr flight and a short 1hr and 30min drive down the coast; you will find yourself arriving in paradise.
