Living for more than 12 years in Playa del Carmen and in love with her paradisiacal and cosmopolitan life. Born and raised in the city of Mexico, Yun has always been very restless and traveling was very young one of her passions.

She lived in the city of Merida for two years and the richness of its fascination culture . Then moved to a small town of 30 thousand inhabitants, because she wanted to know what it was like to live a humble life … only the beach was missing and that’s how Yun arrived in beautiful Playa del Carmen. Starting with tourism, since it is the main activity here and offering 3 languages, Yun really like the interaction with people from all over the world, she learned you take a little of each of them in your heart. That realization opened the panorama to create her own company.
Yun firmly believes that you should always do what you love, life is short and enjoying every day is important. Besides that, what more you can ask for living in a place so similar to paradise.
Why real estate? Rather, it is a job in which you are fulfilling dreams and seeing your customers satisfied is one of the best reasons. In this wonderful Caribbean paradise, for Yun it is the best place in the world.
With two daughters born here and raised in the Riviera Maya, it has shown her how wonderful it is to live in a place surrounded by so much nature and love of animals.
