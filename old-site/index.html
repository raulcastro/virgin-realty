Why Should You Invest in Playa Del Carmen Real Estate?


Playa del Carmen is all about location, location, location. As a travel destination, Playa del Carmen offers scintillating white sand beaches, the crystal clear Caribbean sea and lush jungle all just a short flight from the U.S. All this is found in the centre of the Riviera Maya, making Playa del Carmen the perfect base to explore the natural and cultural wonders of the area. Playa del Carmen is famous for world-class diving, incredible cenotes and lively nightlife. Add to that the unrivalled Mexican hospitality and Playa del Carmen becomes one of the most popular destinations in the world.

The sheer amount of yearly visitors, a weak peso and accessibility have created a strong rental demand. Average occupancy rates can be as high as 80% per year. This demand has in turn developed a low-risk real estate market with multiple ownership options. Investors can choose from beautiful beach front villas and luxury condos that can then be kept for private use or used as a vacation rental. Ownership options include purchasing a private property outright, investing in a fractional ownership or enrolling into a rental pool program.

Playa del Carmen real estate has options to suit every buyer’s needs while generating impressive return on investment. Average ROI for a property in Playa del Carmen can be between 5-10% net with this figure increasing to up to 20% if the property is managed by a professional vacation rental service. Playa del Carmen real estate also enjoys a competitive resale market that is an important factor for long-term investments.

Investing in Playa del Carmen real estate means strong returns with a world-class service in one of the most beautiful destinations in the world.
