Income-Producing Properties

Strong annual appreciation, monthly cash flow, and the opportunity to use your property for your personal enjoyment… purchasing income producing properties in the Riviera Maya is a safe and profitable investment.
Regardless of the size of your investment, there are many opportunities in the Riviera Maya that may fit your needs… Single unit condos for luxury rentals, entire buildings with multiple dwelling units, single family homes, and a wide range of hotels. At this time, Playa del Carmen and Tulum are the two “hot spots”, showing the greatest opportunities and returns.
When you are not enjoying time in Mexico, your property creates the opportunity for substantial rental income. Whether it is short or long-term rentals, property demand remains high and rental projections for the future show substantial growth.
By investing in an income producing property, you do not only enjoy the significant property appreciation over time, but you benefit from substantial monthly cash flow.

Some of the benefits of owning income-producing properties include:

- Conservative investment
- Ability to use your IRA/401K
- Tax benefits and shelter
- A diversified portfolio
- Historical annual appreciation between 8-15%
- Substantial annual rental income

Rent Rolls

Similar to income producing properties, this type of investment is great for generating cash flow. Rent rolls are created a number of ways including acquiring an entire building for the sole purpose of renting the units, developing a condominiums for the sole purpose of renting units out, purchasing blocks of existing condominium buildings, and strategically acquiring units of different sizes in different locations to diversify the roll.

· Lower Overhead and Reduced Management Costs
· Tax Benefits and Shelter
· A Diversified Portfolio

Development

With a booming market and a stream of consistent sales, developing in the Riviera Maya has attracted strong interest from investors over the last 10 years.
There are numerous reasons that developing has become so lucrative here including the fact that not only are labor and materials inexpensive in Mexico, they are also expensed in pesos; the market value is comparable to high-end cities such as Miami; sales transactions are in U.S. dollars; and finally, the USD is extremely strong compared to the MXP.

· Obtainable Construction Loans $1 to 3 Million USD
· Inexpensive Labor and Materials
· Revenue in U.S. Dollars

Joint Venture Partnerships

A joint venture is a business arrangement comprised of two or more parties who combine resources to achieve and specific objective.
Investors looking to minimize their cash outlay, developers seeking investment for new projects, and property owners looking to cash out and dilute their interest are plentiful in the Riviera Maya market.
Virgin Realty has a wide network of clients looking to joint venture with interested investors. One we understand your investment goals, we will introduce you to potential partners in the various market sectors.

Medical Tourism/Outpatient Housing

Medical tourism in Mexico is a vibrant industry that is only growing with popularity. Over one million people come to Mexico for treatment each year to benefit from highly qualified medical professionals, low costs, and its low-stress environment.
Housing for this market is in high demand and Virgin Realty lists many properties in close proximity to local medical facilities.
Renting properties to this growing market is relatively new and thus there are many opportunities to benefit from the medical tourism industry.

Benefits include:

- High ROI
- Consistent occupancy
- Minimal competition
- Rapid industry growth
