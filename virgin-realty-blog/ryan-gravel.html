RYAN GRAVEL

Born and bred in Rhode Island, USA, Ryan was exposed to real estate at a young age due to his family-owned construction company.
Ryan received his B.A. from the University of Central Florida and immediately began working in multiple sectors of the real estate industry including property management, new project consulting, and property sales. Working with many of the top brokerage firms in Florida, Ryan gained invaluable experience that helped shape his vision for the future.
In 2007, Ryan decided it was time to branch out on his own. After doing extensive research on the world’s real estate markets, he identified the Riviera Maya’s potential and decided to move here to make his mark.
Soon after, he formed Virgin Realty Mexico and has not looked back.
Through his strong interpersonal skills, Ryan has developed many key business relationships and has a wide network of clients and suppliers.

Ryan is the managing partner at Virgin. Besides being one the company’s top salespeople, he is responsible for day-to-day company decisions and managing the staff and the business.
Ryan is passionate about what he does and is a true professional. An animal and nature lover, he is also responsible for the company’s Virgin Hearts program – creating events to help protect the local environment and wildlife.
The rare moments Ryan is not working, he can be found on the Playa enjoying paradise with his four dogs.
