Real Estate Services

Virgin Realty Mexico offers a wide array of services that will help simplify the process of buying and selling real estate in the Riviera Maya.

IRA/401k/Retirement Account

You might not be aware that your retirement account may be used to invest in Riviera Maya real estate. Our team will guide you on implementing this investment strategy.


Construction

Our knowledge of the Riviera Maya market, coupled with our extensive relationships, will guide you through the process of purchasing the right property and constructing the building that best suits your business strategy.

Certified Property Appraisals

We will ensure that your properties are accurately appraised.

Consulting

We will educate you on the “in and outs” of investing in the Riviera Maya real estate market. From investment strategy to identifying the best properties to meet your needs - VRM will help you navigate this emerging market.


MLS

There is no Multiple Listing Service in Mexico. VRM has extensive relationships, which allow us to find and list most available properties – often before they hit the open market. All of our listed properties are thoroughly screened before posting.

Mortgages

VRM has access to many lenders. Let us connect you with traditional and alternative lenders throughout the Riviera Maya.

Title Insurance

Ensuring that your property is titled and insured is paramount. VRM only works with the industry’s most credible firms.

Our Commitment

The entire Virgin Realty team is committed to doing all that we can to educate and assist you in finding the right property to meet your needs. We are here to help you through the buying process and to make things as simple as possible. We encourage your questions, and commit to keeping you informed at all times. Our goal is to do everything possible to ensure that you achieve your goals and that your experience with VR Mexico is successful.
