Danielle Evans
Licensed Agent (A.M.P.I)
  • USA/CA Toll free (888) 225 1995 Ext 107

Danielle is originally from Houston, Texas and truly believes you can take the girl out of texas, but you can’t take Texas out of the girl. She has a Bachelor of Arts Degree from Texas State University.

After a 15yr career in mortgage banking, Danielle and is now looking for new ways to help people turn their dreams into reality. After Danielle’s first visit to Playa Del Carmen in January of 2016, she fell in love the city, the culture, and the simpler way of life. She and her husband sold their belongings and moved to Playa full time. They purchased a condo they use as a residence and investment property. They manage it themselves as well.
Most recently, she is now living the life she often fantasized about while fighting traffic in the big city. Whether you are looking for a vacation home, an investment property or maybe both, Danielle has the experience to guide you every step of the way.
