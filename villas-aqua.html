NEIGHBORHOOD

Puerto Aventuras is a luxurious, private gated community nestled on the shores of the Mexican Caribbean Sea, just south of Cancun and downtown Playa del Carmen. Vacationers and residents alike can enjoy the beautiful beaches, while boating enthusiasts will enjoy easy access to a full-service marina and golfers get to play the community’s Tom Lehman-designed signature golf course. Take a stroll around the central commercial area, dive into the community’s popular dolphin experience and check out the fantastic restaurants, shops, and cafes in downtown Puerto Aventuras. The long list of activities to entertain you and your family while visiting is virtually endless: Scuba diving, sailing, parasailing, fishing, paddle boarding, snorkeling and touring the various sites of local Mayan ruins are just a few of the most popular options.
